# SmartBridge SDLC Phase 1: Brainstorming & Ideation

## 1. Idea Prioritization
During initial ideation, three distinct financial technology challenges were evaluated:
1. Automated Credit Card Fraud Detection
2. Smart Lender Loan Eligibility Prediction (Selected)
3. Personal Budgeting AI Assistant

**Selection Rationale:** Loan eligibility prediction directly impacts institutional risk management and credit accessibility. Automated predictive underwriting offers immediate quantifiable ROI for financial institutions.

## 2. Problem Statement
Manual processing of loan applications introduces substantial latency (often 5-10 business days) and subjective operational variance. Financial organizations require an automated, data-driven system to perform instantaneous, objective risk scoring.

## 3. Empathy Map
- **Financial Officer / Underwriter:** Thinks "I need reliable, objective risk assessments without reading through hundreds of documents manually."
- **Loan Applicant:** Thinks "I want fast, transparent decisions on my application without unnecessary waiting periods."
- **System Admin:** Thinks "I need a stable, secure, high-throughput machine learning web service."

## 4. Customer Journey Map
1. **Application Entry:** Applicant navigates to Smart Lender portal on desktop or mobile.
2. **Telemetry Input:** Applicant enters demographic and financial details into validated form fields.
3. **Instant Assessment:** System analyzes credit indicators and renders instantaneous approval probability and status.
4. **Actionable Feedback:** Guidance is provided on parameters influencing creditworthiness.
