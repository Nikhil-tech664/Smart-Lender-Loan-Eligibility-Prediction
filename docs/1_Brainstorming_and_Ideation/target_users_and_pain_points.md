# Target Users and Pain Points

## 1. Target Users

### A. Retail Loan Applicants
- **Role:** Individual applicants applying for consumer, mortgage, or auto credit loans.
- **Goal:** Receive rapid, transparent credit eligibility checks with immediate feedback on how to improve parameters.

### B. Credit Risk Analysts / Underwriters
- **Role:** Financial risk team members validating and overseeing portfolio risk profiles.
- **Goal:** Access robust data telemetries, comparative model accuracy statistics, and automated feature impact heatmaps.

### C. System Administrators / IT DevOps
- **Role:** System operators deploying, monitoring, and scaling the banking application.
- **Goal:** Ensure low latency (<25 ms), zero-error stability, and modular configuration.

---

## 2. Customer Pain Points

| User Role | Current Pain Points | Business Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Loan Applicant** | • Long decision waiting times (5-10 days)<br>• Opaque decisions with zero rationale | • Low satisfaction<br>• High drop-off rate |
| **Credit Underwriter**| • Manual data consolidation and calculation<br>• High rate of human entry errors | • Operational fatigue<br>• Compliance vulnerability |
| **System Admin** | • Monolithic legacy codebases<br>• Difficult deployment and lack of API documentation | • High downtime costs<br>• Blocked DevOps scaling |
