### Detailed Canvas Breakdowns
1. **Customer Segments [CS]:** Primary target is retail home buyers, small business owners, and semiurban borrowers.
2. **Problems & Pains [PR]:** Hard thresholds automatically vetoing applicants with high debt-to-income without checking their savings or asset variables.
3. **Triggers to Act [TR]:** Property purchase deadlines requiring instant pre-qualification letters.
4. **Emotions [EM]:** Shifts the user experience from a black-box bank underwriting model to a transparent, instant AI helper.
5. **Available Solutions [AS]:** Automated web forms, manual loan officer review sheets.
6. **Customer Limitations [CL]:** Restricted access to local branch locations during business hours.
7. **Behavior [BE]:** Seeking multiple quotes simultaneously to compare rates.
8. **Channels of Behavior [CH]:** Accessing the portal on a personal mobile device while reviewing property listings.
9. **Problem Root / Cause [RC]:** Rigid bank algorithms that are not optimized to identify non-traditional qualified borrowers.
10. **Your Solution [SL]:** A web-based classifier providing transparent prediction results and custom limits.
